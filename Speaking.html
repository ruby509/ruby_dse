  • Educational titles teach history or science engagingly.教育遊戲以有趣方式教授歷史或科學。

Reading Books vs Watching Films

Use both mediums for richer stories.

Page + Screen
  • Reading expands vocabulary and imagination.閱讀能擴充詞彙與想像力。
  • Books allow personal pacing and deep perspectives.書籍讓人依步調深入體會觀點。
  • Films deliver visual and emotional impact quickly.電影能迅速帶來視覺與情感震撼。
  • Soundtracks and acting bring stories to life for shared experiences.配樂與演技令故事鮮活並提供共享體驗。
  • Using both formats enriches comprehension.雙管齊下能加深理解。

Developing Work-Life Balance

Plan boundaries to protect joy.

Boundaries = Balance
  • Set boundaries such as no work emails after hours.設定下班後不收工作電郵等界線。
  • Schedule hobbies, exercise, and relationships like important meetings.把興趣、運動與人際關係排入行事曆。
  • Efficient time management prevents tasks spilling into rest.有效時間管理可避免工作侵占休息。
  • Supportive employers and flexible hours sustain balance.支持性的雇主與彈性工時有助維持平衡。

Is Travel Necessary for Education?

Experience cultures firsthand whenever possible.

Go, Grow, Know
  • Travel immerses students in diverse cultures and histories.旅遊讓學生沉浸於多元文化與歷史。
  • Experiencing new lifestyles builds independence and adaptability.體驗新生活方式能建立獨立與適應力。
  • It fosters tolerance and global awareness.旅遊培養寬容與全球視野。

Global Cooperation on Big Problems

Shared crises require shared commitments.

Treaties + Teamwork
  • Agreements like the Paris Accord set shared climate targets.《巴黎協定》等協議訂立共同的氣候目標。
  • Joint research and resource sharing speed up breakthroughs.共同研究與資源共享能加速突破。
  • The UN coordinates efforts and mediates disputes.聯合國協調行動並調解爭端。
  • Funding support for developing nations ensures inclusive progress.對發展中國家的資金支援確保包容的進步。

Globalization’s Impact on Local Cultures

Blend enrichment with preservation.

Open yet Original
  • Globalization introduces new foods, music, and ideas.全球化帶來新飲食、音樂與理念。
  • It enhances cultural exchange and understanding.它促進文化交流與理解。
  • Dominant cultures can overshadow local languages and crafts.主流文化可能掩蓋本地語言與工藝。
  • Preservation efforts must accompany openness to stay unique.在保持開放同時必須保存才能維持獨特性。

Role of the United Nations in Peace

Reform and resources keep peacekeeping relevant.

Mediate • Protect • Reform
  • The UN offers diplomatic platforms to defuse conflicts.聯合國提供外交平台化解衝突。
  • Peacekeepers protect civilians in unstable regions.維和部隊在動盪地區保護平民。
  • It promotes human rights and coordinates humanitarian aid.它推廣人權並統籌人道援助。
  • Reforms are needed to overcome veto limits and funding gaps.仍需改革以克服否決權限制與資金缺口。
